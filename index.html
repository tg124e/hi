What is youtube views? 

YouTube views refer to the number of times a video has been watched by viewers on the YouTube platform. When someone clicks on a video and watches it for a certain period of time, it is counted as a view. The view count is an important metric used to gauge the popularity and reach of a video. It provides an indication of how many people have seen the content and can be used to measure the success or impact of a video. YouTube views are publicly displayed on the video's page and can influence the video's ranking and visibility on the platform.

What is youtube watch hours?

YouTube watch hours, also known as watch time, refers to the total duration of time that viewers have spent watching videos on a particular YouTube channel. It is measured by adding up the lengths of all the videos watched by viewers. Watch hours are a crucial metric for YouTube creators as they play a significant role in channel growth, monetization, and visibility.

YouTube introduced a policy in 2018 that requires channels to accumulate a minimum of 4,000 watch hours within the last 12 months in order to be eligible for monetization through the YouTube Partner Program. Alongside the watch hour requirement, channels must also have at least 1,000 subscribers. This policy was implemented to ensure that channels have a dedicated and engaged audience before they can earn revenue from advertisements on their videos.

The watch hours metric is an important indicator of a channel's popularity, engagement, and content quality. It helps YouTube assess the overall performance of a channel and determine its eligibility for monetization and other features. Additionally, watch hours play a role in YouTube's algorithm, influencing video recommendations and search rankings.

Tips to get views on youtube

Compelling Thumbnails: Create eye-catching thumbnails that accurately represent your video's content. A visually appealing thumbnail can grab viewers' attention and entice them to click on your video.

Engaging Titles: Craft descriptive and intriguing titles that clearly convey what your video is about. Incorporate keywords relevant to your content to improve searchability.

Quality Content: Focus on creating high-quality videos that provide value to your target audience. Make sure your videos are well-edited, visually appealing, and deliver on what you promise in the title and thumbnail.

Optimize Video Descriptions: Write detailed and keyword-rich descriptions for your videos. Include relevant links, timestamps, and a brief summary of the content. This helps both viewers and YouTube's algorithm understand your video better.

Tags and Keywords: Utilize relevant tags and keywords that accurately reflect your video's topic. This can improve the discoverability of your videos in YouTube's search results and related videos section.

Video Promotion: Share your videos on your social media channels, website, or blog to increase exposure. Engage with your audience by responding to comments and encouraging them to share your videos with others.

Collaborations and Cross-Promotion: Collaborate with other YouTubers or creators in your niche. This can help you tap into their audience and gain exposure to new viewers. Additionally, consider cross-promoting each other's videos on your respective channels.

Consistent Upload Schedule: Establish a regular schedule for uploading videos. Consistency helps build anticipation among your audience and encourages them to return for new content.

Engage with Your Audience: Respond to comments, ask for feedback, and encourage viewers to subscribe, like, and share your videos. Building a loyal community can lead to increased views and audience engagement.

YouTube SEO: Understand YouTube's algorithm and optimize your videos for search. Use relevant keywords, engaging titles, and accurate descriptions to improve your chances of ranking higher in search results.

Remember, building an audience and increasing views takes time and effort. Consistently creating high-quality content, optimizing your videos, and engaging with your audience will contribute to the growth of your YouTube channel.
